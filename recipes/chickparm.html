F&J's Famous Chicken Parmesan

Description

This is one of F & J's favorite recipes to make, and the best part is how easy it is to do! The chicken parmesan is best when it's eaten right out of the oven, while the cheese is still melty and warm. Take a look at the simple ingredients and steps needed to make this awesome dish at home!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast (thinly sliced)
  • Mozzarella Cheese (thick slices)
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Flour
  • Parmesan Cheese (grated)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Coat the Chicken Breast with flour, egg, and Panko Bread Crumbs
  3. Sear coated Chicken Breasts in frying pan for 4-5 minutes each side
  4. Place coated Chicken Breasts in baking sheet
  5. Pour Marinara Sauce over Chicken Breasts
  6. Place Mozzarella Cheese slices over each Chicken Breast
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes @ 400 degrees F
  8. Add garnish to taste, and enjoy!
