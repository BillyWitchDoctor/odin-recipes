Beer

Description

Beer was the earliest alcoholic drink created by humans, dating back over 10,000 years ago. Luckily for us, it's still around and is easier than ever to make at your home! Follow our easy-to-use guide and you'll find yourself cruisin' and boozin' in no time at all!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of cold beer
  • 1 hand to hold the can
  • 1 hand to open the can
  • 1 hand to rule them all, and in the darkness bind them

Steps

  1. Open the beer
  2. Drink the beer
  3. Enjoy!
