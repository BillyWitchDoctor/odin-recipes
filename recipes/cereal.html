World's Best Cereal

Description

Remember waking up on Saturday mornings as a kid? The first memories that come to my mind usually involve a nice big bowl of cereal while watching cartoons. It doesn't even matter that my mom would rarely let us have the so called sugar cereals (you know, the best kinds of cereal?). Take a trip down memory lane with this delicious and nutri- .... ahem, well this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • Box of Cereal (more sugar = more better)
  • Milk (% fat to your taste)
  • 1 Bowl
  • The biggest spoon your mom will let you use

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into the bowl
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Allow milk to soak until preferred sogginess is achieved
  4. Put on Loony Tunes and enjoy your cereal!
